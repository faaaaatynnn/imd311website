Trailer Movie Cinta Subuh

Angga, a student who struggles to pray on time, develops feelings for the erudite and devout Ratih.
Ratih was moved to give Angga her heart because of her individuality, genuineness, joy, and disposition.
Rey Mbayang and Dinda Hauw, a husband and wife cast, performed an outstanding performance,
and they worked together with Roger Danuarta to make a popular movie.
